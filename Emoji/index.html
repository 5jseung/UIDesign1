The Story

Inspired by the story of the dumpling emoji, I decided to create a soju emoji. As a Korean, I noticed that while there are many alcohol emojis available, none capture the iconic design of soju bottles. Soju is an integral part of Korean culture and is also gaining popularity worldwide. Its distinctive green bottle design is not only recognizable in Korea but also across cultures, resembling similar designs like those of sake and certain beer bottles .

The soju emoji aims to represent this cultural staple in digital conversations, offering a way for people to share and celebrate Korean culture. With its growing international presence, the soju emoji can also appeal to a global audience, connecting people through a shared appreciation of this unique beverage.
